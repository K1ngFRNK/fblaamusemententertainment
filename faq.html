We've answered some questions you might have been thinking..

Can I record on decices while on rides?

Nope. To ensure the safety of everyone in FBLA Amusement and Entertainment, photography/recording devices are not allowed while on rides (Violation of this privacy is subject to removal).

What are the security measures of FBLA Amusement and Entertainment?

Guests arriving will go through metal detectors. All bags of anykind will be subject to inspection prior to entry. Security staff will be placed around the area to keep everything safe. There will be report desks in every area in case there was an incident that needs reporting.

What is the 'Weather Wrap-up' protocol?

Under certain weather conditions, some rides will close. Specifically rain, high-winds, and lightning/thunder.

Are pets allowed at FBLA Amusement and Entertainment?

FBLA Amusement and Entertainment also has a park area where pets are allowed. On the riding grounds, only service dogs are permitted to be on the grounds.

What procedures are in place to minimize the spread of disease and illnesses?

Extensive training for associates on maintaining a safe and hygienic workplace and environment for our guests, Appropriate personal protective clothing and gear for associates who are assigned for cleaning duties, Daily sanitation procedures, for everything from bathroom faucets and sinks to kitchen and dining surfaces, benches and trash cans, Daily cleaning and “wash down” of outdoors locations, including walkways and queue lines, Guest and associate first aid locations to assist with minor health issues.
